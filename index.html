About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Cornell Tech (Cornell University), working as a Research Associate under the supervision of Professor Jae-sun Seo in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. My research area focuses on quantization, pruning, and neural architecture search for neuromorphic computing-based hardware applications.

I have recently completed my Master's degree from Government College University, Lahore, and hold a Bachelor's degree from COMSATS University Islamabad, Pakistan.
